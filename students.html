For Students

Edudatagie is not just for education professionals-students and parents can benefit from our services too! Whether your child is struggling or bored in school, Edudatagie provides exceptional research-based support in your home or at your local library (North and Central New Jersey), or on Skype worldwide.

Edudatagie also offers assistance with college admissions for domestic and international students wishing to pursue their higher education in the U.S.

  • Private tutoring for students K-6
  • On-call homework help
  • Writing help for high school and college students
  • College admissions counseling (Includes International applicants)
